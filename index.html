Pune, known as Poona until 1978 is the second largest city in the state of Maharashtra and the 7th most populous city in India, with an estimated population of 7.4 million as of 2020. It has been ranked as the most livable city in India several times. Pune is widely regarded as the second major IT hub of India and the top automobile and manufacturing hub of India. Pune is situated at approximately 18° 32' north latitude and 73° 51' east longitude. Pune lies on the western margin of the Deccan plateau, at an altitude of 560 m (ft) above sea level.
